What is DAIBETES about?

DAIBETES is an experimental project designed to assist people with type 1 diabetes in managing their condition and maintaining blood sugar levels. The final aim of the project is to be able to accurately predict insulin dosage based on various user information, such as their alimentation (Openfood Fact API-based), their blood sugar levels (based on FreeStyle Libre data), how they feel.

Why am I developing this application?

I have often been called a 'bad' type 1 diabetic, and I struggle to count everything I eat. I am also passionate about AI in healthcare and its multiple facets.

About me

I am currently working as a Data Scientist at Guys and St Thomas NHS Foundation Trust, as part of the Clinical Scientific Team. My area of expertise is Natural Language Processing, that I apply on a daily basis to tackle Gastroenterology problems. My background is in Computer Science, with a specialization in AI and machine learning as well as in Bioengineering.
